My Story

From coursework to production code.

I'm an Artificial Intelligence undergraduate at COMSATS University Islamabad, Attock Campus, currently maintaining a 3.76/4.0 CGPA. What started as curiosity about how intelligent systems work turned into a habit of building — first small scripts, then full applications.

Today my work sits at the intersection of three things: clean full-stack engineering, thoughtful interface design in Flutter, and applied machine learning. I've built a CRM system with role-based access, designed mobile interfaces for food and student-management apps, and trained classification models on real HR data.

I care about finishing what I start. It's easy to prototype something that looks good in a demo — the harder, more valuable skill is shipping something that still works a month later. That's the standard I hold my own projects to.

Outside of coursework, I attend AI community events around Attock (like GDG Attock's "For the Love of AI"), and I'm currently looking for opportunities where I can grow as an engineer while contributing meaningfully from day one.
